EDGAR Here, father, take the shadow of this tree For your good host; pray that the right may thrive: If ever I return to you again, I'll bring you comfort. GLOUCESTER Grace go with you, sir! EDGAR Away, old man; give me thy hand; away! King Lear hath lost, he and his daughter ta'en: Give me thy hand; come on. GLOUCESTER No farther, sir; a man may rot even here. EDGAR What, in ill thoughts again? Men must endure Their going hence, even as their coming hither; Ripeness is all: come on. GLOUCESTER
