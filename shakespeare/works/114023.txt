AGAMEMNON Hark! hark! what shout is that? NESTOR Peace, drums! Achilles! Achilles! Hector's slain! Achilles. DIOMEDES The bruit is, Hector's slain, and by Achilles. AJAX If it be so, yet bragless let it be; Great Hector was a man as good as he. AGAMEMNON March patiently along: let one be sent To pray Achilles see us at our tent.
